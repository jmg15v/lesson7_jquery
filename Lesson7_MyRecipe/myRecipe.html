Peanut Butter Fudge

Ingredients

  • 2 c sugar
  • 3 tbs cocoa powder
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 tbs vanilla
  • 1/2 c peanuut butter

Instructions

In a medium sauce pan, add sugar, cocoa, butter, and milk

Turn burner on medium-high

Stir constantly and wait for it to start boiling

Once boiling, allow to boil (while stirring) for 2 minutes

Take pan off of the burner

Immediately add vanilla and peanut butter

Stir well, mixture should be thickening

Pour into a buttered pan to cool
